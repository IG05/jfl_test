What is Blue-Green Deployment?

Blue-green deployment is a technique that reduces downtime and risk by running two identical production environments called Blue and Green. At any time, only one environment is live, serving all production traffic.

1

Zero Downtime

Switch between environments instantly without service interruption. Your users never experience downtime during deployments.

2

Instant Rollback

If issues arise, immediately switch back to the previous stable environment with a single click.

3

Risk Reduction

Test in production-like environment before switching traffic, minimizing deployment risks.
